We've Updated Our Password Requirements!

We now follow the latest NIST cybersecurity guidelines which focus on password length and avoiding common passwords, rather than complex character requirements.

This means: Longer, memorable passwords are better than short complex ones!

üìã Simple Password Requirements

Your password only needs to meet these requirements:

  • Between 8-64 characters
    Longer passwords are more secure than complex short ones
  • Not a commonly used password
    Avoid passwords like "password123", "qwerty", etc.
  • No excessive repetition
    Avoid patterns like "aaaaaaa" or "1111111"
